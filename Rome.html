Hotel Arcangelo 7.9



Hotel Arcangelo enjoys a quiet location just a few streets behind Castel Sant’Angelo. Set in a completely restored building in an Art Nouveau style, it features a roof garden with views of nearby Saint Peter’s Basilica. All rooms are en suite. The Arcangelo is the only 3-star hotel of the area provided with a free outdoor parking. A garage is also available nearby. Communal areas are elegant, and the rooms all come with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby. A generous sweet and savoury buffet is served daily in the breakfast room or directly in your room. Drinks are served on the terrace. In the surrounding residential area you will find great shopping streets and numerous cafés and restaurants. The Vatican is a 10-minute walk away, as is Piazza Navona, across the river. The property is 100 m from the airport shuttle bus stop. This is our guests' favourite part of Rome, according to independent reviews. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.3 for a two-person trip.

Roma Luxus Hotel 8.4



Roma Luxus Hotel offers free WiFi and stylish rooms in Rome's Monti district. Set 300 m from the Roman Forum, the property also features a bar and an à la carte restaurant. Rooms at the Luxus Hotel Roma are all spacious and come with a flat-screen TV, electric kettle and air conditioning. Each room includes a private bathroom with bathrobes and slippers. An extensive sweet and savoury international buffet breakfast is available daily. It includes eggs, pancakes, cheese and pastries. The hotel is 400 m from both Piazza Venezia and Quirinale Palace, while Via Nazionale shopping street can be reached in 1 minute on foot. Rome Ciampino Airport is 15 km away. This is our guests' favourite part of Rome, according to independent reviews. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.5 for a two-person trip.

Hotel Eden - Dorchester Collection 9.4



Boasting a rooftop terrace and set in one of Rome's most exclusive areas, near Via Veneto street, Hotel Eden - Dorchester Collection offers luxury rooms, A Michelin-starred restaurant, and free WiFi throughout. The Spanish Steps are 550 m away. With classic Italian furnishings, each room here includes a satellite LED TV, a minibar, and air conditioning. Most rooms have city views. The award-winning La Terrazza restaurant with panoramic views serves gourmet Mediterranean cuisine for dinner, matched with the finest of wines. Il Giardino Ristorante, is open for both lunch and dinner. Breakfast is an extensive buffet. The Villa Borghese park is 350 m away, while the Trevi Fountain can be reached on foot in 15 minutes. This is our guests' favourite part of Rome, according to independent reviews. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.4 for a two-person trip.
