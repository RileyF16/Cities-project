Habtoor Palace Dubai, LXR Hotels & Resorts 8.9



Located on the arterial Sheikh Zayed Road, the Habtoor Palace Dubai, LXR Hotels & Resorts offers grand accommodation in Al Habtoor City, Dubai’s first-ever integrated urban resort. The hotel sits on the banks of the breathtaking Dubai Water Canal. Guests can refresh and unwind at either two outdoor rooftop swimming pools set amidst lush greenery. Free WiFi is available throughout the property. All spacious rooms in the Habtoor Palace Dubai are elegantly decorated, and the hotel also offers 52 suites, including its flagship Sir Winston Churchill Suite and its Bentley Suite. Each includes a flat screen TV, a minibar, walk in closet and a spacious bathroom. All guests have access to butler service. Habtoor Palace Dubai offers 7 distinctive dining venues, including French restaurant BQ - Kitchen & Bar and the award-winning prime cut restaurant, World Cut Steakhouse. Guests can also enjoy the Grand Afternoon Tea at Sidra. Habtoor Palace Dubai also offers a Silk Spa where guests can enjoy treatments in any of the six treatment rooms (three male, three female), two Hammams, and separate female and male wet areas that include steam room and sauna. Guests can also utilize the fitness centre on site or access to the beach of sister property Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah; complimentary for all Habtoor Palace Dubai guests, conveniently accessible with the hotel's daily shuttle service. The hotel also offers outdoor and indoor event spaces including a ballroom that can host up to 700 guests as well as and 7 fully equipped meeting rooms. Next door to the Habtoor Palace Dubai, LXR Hotels & Resorts lies the spectacular Las Vegas style Theatre of La Perle inspired by the city of Dubai. Just minutes away from major city attractions such as Burj Khalifa, The Dubai Mall, Business Bay, DIFC, and Al Farooq Omar Bin Al Khattab Mosque, a 20-minute drive to Dubai International Airport and 40-minute drive to Al Maktoum International Airport, the hotel is strategically located within easy access from the city’s many hotspots. Free valet parking is available on site. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip.

Atlantis, The Palm 4.5/5



Located on Dubai’s Palm Jumeirah Island and enjoys a private sandy beach, the 5-star Atlantis offers stunning views of the Arabian Gulf. It provides an underwater aquarium and complimentary access to the Aquaventure water park and the Lost Chambers Aquarium. Free WiFi is available throughout the entire property. All the Arabian and oceanic décor inspired rooms feature a balcony with scenic views. They include a flat-screen satellite TV, a seating area, complimentary tea/coffee making facilities and a mini bar. Some rooms include a stunning large terrace with panoramic views of the palm or an over sized spa bath located in the center of the bathroom. Dining options include Hakkasan, which serves Authentic Cantonese cuisine in a modern setting. The award-winning Nobu restaurant serves contemporary Japanese cuisine with Arabian influences. Bread Street Kitchen Bar offers signature British menu and delightful beverages from Gordon Ramsay. Guests can enjoy stunning views of The Palm and Dubai skyline while savoring the tastes of authentic Lebanese cuisine along with delicious beverages at Ayamna Guests enjoy complimentary access to Aquaventure, the largest waterpark in the Middle East, features the Tower of Neptune and the Tower of Poseidon packed with thrilling water slides. A water playground for younger guests is available. The Kids Club provides the best fully supervised entertainment for your children and with daily programs. The Zone offers teens a chance to hangout, dance and play the latest video games. Wavehouse offers both dining and entertainment in one area where guests can enjoy good food and drinks, a play area for children and adults, a four lane bowling alley, a state of the art wave machine and more. Guests can enjoy facial and body treatments at the ShuiQi Spa. The gym provides personal fitness coaches. The property offers a range of some of the world’s finest high end boutiques and shops that cater for all of your holiday essentials. Guests have the opportunity to book a helicopter ride for a 15 minute tour around Dubai or for Airport transportation. Mall of the Emirates can be reached in 20 minutes by car. The Emirates Golf Club is 3 km away. The iconic Burj Khalifa and The Dubai Mall are a 25-minute drive from the Atlantis. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.2 for a two-person trip.
