Best Western Norwalk Inn 8.3



Just off Interstate 605, within a short drive of Commerce Casino, this 100% non-smoking hotel in Norwalk, California offers a free continental breakfast every morning and free wired high-speed internet access. An outdoor pool is available. Guest rooms at the property offer cable flat-screen TVs, a refrigerator, a microwave, and a work desk. A hairdryer, an iron with ironing facilities, and complimentary toiletries are also standard. Best Western Norwalk Inn features convenient amenities such as free on-site parking. Attractions such as Knott's Berry Farm amusement park and the Bicycle Club Card Casino are with driving distance of the Norwalk Best Western. Guests can also go shopping at the nearby Cerritos Mall, then see the Medieval Times dinner show. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.3 for a two-person trip.

Hotel Sofitel Los Angeles at Beverly Hills 8.0



Sofitel Los Angeles at Beverly Hills is a stunning 4-diamonds AAA hotel in LA offering elegant luxury accommodations and dramatic decor with an ambiance that combines see-and-be-seen excitement and the calm of an urban resort. Named by Conde Nast Traveler® as a top hotel in Los Angeles in their annual Reader’s Choice Awards, Sofitel Los Angeles artfully blends Hollywood Glamour and French Flair for an incomparable hospitality experience. Ideally situated on the corner of La Cienega and Beverly Boulevard, opposite the best shopping destination in Los Angeles the Beverly Center, this hotel is conveniently located near Cedar Sinai Medical Center, Rodeo Drive, Melrose Avenue, Sunset Boulevard, West Hollywood’s Santa Monica Boulevard and The Grove, just a couple minutes away from the best shopping spots and tourist attractions of Los Angeles, West Hollywood and Beverly Hills. 16.1 km from LAX / 19.3 km from Burbank Airport / 14.5 km from Union Station. Banks, grocery stores, car rentals, postal services and much more at walking distance. This elegant hotel offers 295 rooms on 10 floors including 26 suites and two Presidential Suites all featuring Sofitel's luxurious signature bedding and state-of-the-art technology, as well as luxurious French bath amenities and rain showers. Room amenities include SoBed – Sofitel’s luxurious feathertop and duvet sleep system, 47” TV’s, Wi-Fi, 24 hour In-room dining, minibar, safe deposit box, movies on demand. The onsite wellness amenities offer a 360 ° wellness experience featuring an outdoor heated pool, Sofitel Fitness, a 2,000-square-foot contemporary and exclusive fitness facility landscaped by cardio and free-weight workout systems, a spa offering a wide array of indulgent cosmetology treatments and Le Salon, a celebrity hair salon offering exclusive beauty glamour moments. Guests can enjoy “ALL Be Well”, the hotel new exclusive restorative in-room wellness program in partnership with Three Sages, a leader in immersive, nature-based restorative experiences.

Hyatt Regency Huntington Beach Resort and Spa 8.9



Adjacent to the beach, Hyatt Regency Huntington Beach Resort and Spa offers surf and paddle boarding lessons. Two outdoor pools and spa services are available for guests. Several dining options are provided on site. A flat-screen satellite TV with pay-per-view channels is included in each air-conditioned room at Hyatt Regency Huntington Beach Resort and Spa. Each guest room includes a patio or a balcony. Showcasing views of the Pacific Ocean, WATERTABLE features a gorgeous dining space that offers a Gastro Pantry and Bar with breakfast by day and cocktails by night. Featuring fish tacos and beach-inspired cocktails, Pete’s Sunset Grille is open every day for lunch and dinner. Located right across from lifeguard station 15, Tower 15 Pizzeria offers pizza, salads and sandwiches. Guests can work out in the on-site fitness centre or relax at the spa with a massage or facial. A games room, a playground and a children’s pool with waterslides are located on site. Bike hires are available. Meadowlark Golf Course is 12 km away from this resort. Disneyland is 35 km away. A seasonal shuttle to the theme park is available. Los Angeles International Airport is 44 minutes’ drive away. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.4 for a two-person trip.
